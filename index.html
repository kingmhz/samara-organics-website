01 — OUR ORIGIN

THE MURRAH BUFFALO LEGACY

Where pure A2 milk
meets organic care.

Born in the fertile dairy belt between Bulandshahr and Aligarh, Samara Organics raises purebred Murrah buffaloes — the world's finest dairy breed, naturally producing A2 protein-rich milk.

Our Murrah herd is raised organically on open pastures with no hormones, no antibiotics, and no shortcuts. Every glass of Samara milk carries the full nutritional richness of A2 beta-casein — easier to digest, kinder to the body, and true to tradition.

We combine the region's generations-old dairy knowledge with careful testing, hygienic handling and a transparent local supply chain. From our farm to your family — every drop is traceable.

OUR PHILOSOPHY ↗

Black Gold: The Murrah Buffalo

Originating from the fertile lands of northern India, the purebred Murrah buffalo is globally celebrated as the premier dairy breed.

A2 BETA-CASEIN Produces 100% A2 protein-rich milk, naturally light and easy on the digestion system.
PREMIUM CREAM & SNF Yields naturally high fat (6.8%+), creating unmatched thick textures and sweet aroma.
NATURAL GRAZING Raised organically on certified pastures under gentle care, free of synthetic hormones.

Our Heritage 5-Step Bilona Method

We slow-craft our signature ghee using the ancient Bilona method to preserve nutrition, flavor, and texture.

01

Organic Milking

Pure A2 milk is collected at dawn from our organic feed-grown Murrah herd.

02

Traditional Curd

Milk is boiled and set into slow-set dahi overnight in clay vessels.

03

Wooden Churning

Curd is churned bi-directionally with a wooden bilona to separate fresh butter (Makhan).

04

Slow Clay Simmering

Makhan is simmered on a low fire until it clarifies into pure liquid gold.

05

Granular Cooling

Ghee is slowly cooled in glass jars, resulting in the desired granular structure and aroma.
