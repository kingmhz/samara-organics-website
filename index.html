Our Heritage 5-Step Bilona Method

We slow-craft our signature ghee using the ancient Bilona method to preserve nutrition, flavor, and texture.

01

Organic Milking

Milk is collected under a documented hygiene routine from the Samara Murrah herd.

02

Traditional Curd

Milk is boiled and set into slow-set dahi overnight in clay vessels.

03

Wooden Churning

Curd is churned bi-directionally with a wooden bilona to separate fresh butter (Makhan).

04

Slow Clay Simmering

Makhan is simmered on a low fire until it clarifies into pure liquid gold.

05

Granular Cooling

Ghee is slowly cooled in glass jars, resulting in the desired granular structure and aroma.

STANDARDSAMARA APPROACH
Sourcing

Direct, identified farm batches rather than anonymous pooled supply.

Testing

Batch records for core quality and safety checks before dispatch.

Short local routes planned around Aligarh, Bulandshahr and Khurja.

Animal care

Clean housing, suitable feed, veterinary oversight and low-stress handling.

A2

Milk identified by its beta-casein protein type. It is not a medical treatment and does not make milk safe for anyone with a milk allergy.

Bilona

A traditional process in which cultured curd is churned to butter and the butter is slowly clarified into ghee.

Organic

A regulated production claim that should only appear on sale packaging once the relevant certification and records are in place.

Batch tested

A defined production lot linked to recorded checks. The exact tests, method, laboratory and result should be shown on its batch page.
