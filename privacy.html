Privacy
policy.

Effective 12 July 2026

This policy explains what information the Samara Organics website uses, where it stays, and what happens when you choose to contact us.

01

Information you choose to provide

You may enter a mobile number, delivery PIN code, or select products for an enquiry. The current website keeps these details in your browser’s local storage to remember your choices. They are not automatically transmitted to our server.

02

WhatsApp enquiries

When you select “Continue on WhatsApp” or confirm the first-delivery list, the website opens WhatsApp with a message prepared from the details you entered. Nothing is sent until you review and send that message yourself. WhatsApp’s own privacy terms then apply.

03

Technical data

The website uses a service worker and browser cache to improve speed and offline reliability. It also loads typefaces from Google Fonts, which may receive standard connection information such as your IP address and browser details.

04

How we use messages

Information you send to us may be used to answer questions, understand launch demand, plan delivery routes, and contact you about Samara Organics products. We do not sell your personal information.

05

Your choices

You can clear saved product selections, mobile details and PIN-code information by clearing site data in your browser. You may also ask us to delete information previously sent to us by contacting the email address below.

06

Contact

For privacy questions, email samaraorganics.india@gmail.com or call +91 80773 66897.
