Privacy
policy.

Effective 12 July 2026

This policy explains the information used to provide Samara Organics orders, subscriptions, quality verification and customer support.

01

Information we collect

When you order, subscribe or submit a support request, we may collect your name, mobile number, email, delivery address, PIN code, chosen products, quantities, delivery schedule, payment information, issue category, message and related order reference. Messages sent through WhatsApp, email or telephone are also handled through those channels.

02

How the website stores information

The website may remember your cart, name, mobile number, address and PIN code in your browser’s local storage. Completed website orders and subscriptions are also transmitted to and stored in the Samara Organics order database so that we can fulfil and support them.

03

How we use information

We use this information to confirm orders, manage subscriptions, plan routes, deliver products, reconcile payments, answer questions, prevent misuse and maintain business records. We do not sell personal information.

04

Payments and third-party services

Payment references may be stored for reconciliation. When production payment services, WhatsApp, email, hosting or messaging providers are used, their own privacy terms apply to information processed through them. Full card or bank credentials should never be entered into Samara Organics text fields.

05

Security and access

Administrative order data is restricted to authenticated staff. We use access controls, request validation and transport security appropriate to the service. No internet system can be guaranteed completely secure, so production systems are monitored, updated and backed up.

06

Retention and your choices

Order and subscription records may be retained for fulfilment, accounting, dispute resolution and applicable legal obligations. You may clear browser-saved data through your browser settings and request access, correction or deletion of eligible information by contacting us.

07

Technical services

The website uses a service worker and browser cache for speed and offline reliability. Its typefaces are served from the Samara Organics website rather than requested from a third-party font provider. Production analytics, if later added, will be disclosed here.

08

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, use the customer-care portal, email samaraorganics.india@gmail.com or call +91 80773 66897.
