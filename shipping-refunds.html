CUSTOMER CARE

Delivery &
refunds.

Effective 12 July 2026

Fresh dairy has a short, careful journey. This policy explains how we handle delivery problems, changes and refunds while protecting product safety.

01

Delivery area and timing

Delivery is available only on confirmed Samara Organics routes. Slots are preferences and may shift because of weather, traffic, production or animal-care requirements. We will communicate material delays using the contact details supplied with your order.

02

Receiving perishable products

Please ensure someone can receive and refrigerate dairy promptly. If access is unavailable or contact attempts fail, a perishable order may be returned or safely disposed of and may not qualify for a refund.

03

Damaged, incorrect or unsafe products

Contact us as soon as possible after delivery with the order number, product, issue and photographs where relevant. Do not consume a product if its seal is broken, packaging is swollen, temperature appears unsafe or the product seems contaminated.

04

Cancellations

One-time orders may be cancelled before the communicated preparation or route cut-off. Because dairy is perishable and prepared against demand, cancellation may not be possible after processing begins. Subscription changes should be requested before the next delivery cut-off.

05

Refund review

Where Samara Organics confirms non-delivery, an incorrect item, a quality defect or damage attributable to fulfilment, we may replace the product, issue account credit or return the eligible amount through the original or agreed payment method. Processing time depends on the payment provider.

06

Non-returnable food

For food-safety reasons, delivered dairy cannot normally be physically returned or resold. A refund is not available solely for personal taste preference, incorrect address, unavailable recipient or storage after delivery, unless consumer law requires otherwise.

07

Subscriptions

A customer with a private management link can pause, resume, skip eligible future dates, change quantity or address, and cancel. Changes apply only before the displayed route or preparation cut-off. A cancelled subscription cannot be restored online; support can create a new one after confirming availability.

08

Consumer rights and grievances

Nothing in this policy limits rights available under applicable Indian consumer law. Our online sale disclosures and grievance process are intended to follow the Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, 2020 and the 2021 amendment. See the Department of Consumer Affairs source. Before commercial launch, Samara must publish its legal entity name, GST/FSSAI details where applicable, grievance officer details and a working complaint acknowledgement process.

09

Support

Submit and track a request through our customer-care portal, or contact +91 80773 66897 or samaraorganics.india@gmail.com and include your order or subscription number.
